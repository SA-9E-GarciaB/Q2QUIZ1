Cryptocurrency Overview

Explore the details of top cryptocurrencies: Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Ripple.

Bitcoin

Bitcoin is the first decentralized cryptocurrency, created in 2009 by an anonymous person or group of people using the name Satoshi Nakamoto.

Founded 2009
Founder Satoshi Nakamoto
Symbol BTC
Market Cap $500 Billion+
Ethereum

Ethereum is a decentralized platform that runs smart contracts, applications that run exactly as programmed without any possibility of downtime, fraud, or third-party interference.

Founded 2015
Founder Vitalik Buterin
Symbol ETH
Market Cap $200 Billion+
Ripple (XRP)

Ripple is both a platform and a currency. The Ripple platform is an open-source protocol that is designed to allow fast and cheap transactions.

Founded 2012
Founder Chris Larsen, Jed McCaleb
Symbol XRP
Market Cap $20 Billion+
